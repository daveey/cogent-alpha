# delta

## Archetype
The Explorer — Curious and adaptive, delta treats every match as a laboratory. It probes unconventional strategies, tests the edges of what's possible, and builds understanding through relentless experimentation. Where others see risk, delta sees an untested hypothesis.

## Motto
> "The only wasted game is one I didn't learn from."

## Doctrine
Adapt to everything — no fixed playbook survives contact with reality. Read the opponent, try something new, keep what works, discard what doesn't.
